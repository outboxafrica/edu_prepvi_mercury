My Profile

Early Life

I was born, the 7th of 8 children of my parents;Vicent and Valerie Mutumba. Our home is located in Buddo, Wakiso district.
I fondly remember many happy moments and some testing ones. Let me give you an example:
I learnt making crafts like mats etc from my mother who was so good at it and , I too had perfected the art.
Whenever I went away to play, my siblings would say to me that “your mat has been crying for you as you were gone”.
I would then feel bad and throw away that mat and start a fresh new one. In so doing, I ended up having no particular mat or craft to show!
There are many more fond memories of my early days which I can share but because of time, let me stop at that.

Education

I started Nursary school at a school called Milly and Martin Nursary school in Buddo. I then joined Buddo Preparatory School where I found sharp pupils. In the school term that followed, my class teacher loudly made a remark about my performance in class.
She said, “Busuulwa, will you be sweeping the road as...(and she mentioned a name of one of my classmates)..drive her car passed you? It so happened that the name of the classmate she mentioned was the same girl I secretly admired.
It was an insult to me,so I resolved to do my very best. I raised my standard that I always came among the top 10 students in class.
It therefore followed that at the final national primary exams, I got Distinction 2 grade in each of the four exams sat.(The very best students would get a distinction 1 grade in each of the exam).
